Frequently Asked Questions

What are the rules for posting on The Student Voice?

We encourage respectful and constructive discussions. Posts should be relevant to student life, education, or campus events. Avoid hate speech, personal attacks, and spam. Full guidelines can be found in our Terms of Service.

How can I report inappropriate content or users?

If you come across content or users that violate our community guidelines, please use the "Report" button next to the post or on the user's profile. Our moderation team will review the report and take appropriate action.

What types of content are not allowed on the platform?

Content that is illegal, discriminatory, or harmful is strictly prohibited. This includes but is not limited to: copyright infringement, personal information sharing without consent, explicit content, and promotion of dangerous activities.

How can I improve the visibility of my blog posts?

To increase your post's visibility, use relevant tags, engage with other users' content, share your posts on social media, and consistently produce high-quality content. Remember to follow our guidelines for best practices in content creation.
